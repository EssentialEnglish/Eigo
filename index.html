Beneficiaries

Everyone can benefit from this platform.

Tutors

Tutors don't have to be shackled by corporate curriculums anymore. Upload your own materials and charge your own rates.

Learners

Learners can chose from a wider range of topics and tutors at lower prices. The sky's the limit!

Free Speech

As a free speech platform tutors and learners are free from corporate agendas.

Timeline

We are confident we will make it to the moon.

  • Stage 1

    LBRY Fork

    We plan to fork the LBRY UI to target Japanese English language learners and English language tutors.

    Tutors will be able to publish their own educational material and charge their own rates. Learners will be able to choose their prefered tutor, subject and materials without commiting to a lengthy and expensive corporate curriculum.

    With categories focusing on General Conversation, Exams, Literature or Business and other subject areas, tutors and learners can focus on their chosen area of expertise.

An optional free download will benefit learners by providing free materials and tutors with free advertising while the option to maintain a paywall will ensure tutors an income for their hard work.

The paid video call appointment section will allow tutors to make a living and provide learners with the chance to learn one-on-one with their favourite content creators.

  • Stage 2

    Marketing to English language learners in Japan and tutors worldwide.
