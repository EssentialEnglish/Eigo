Timeline

We are confident that we will make it to the moon eventually.


  • Stage One

    LBRY Fork

    We plan to fork the LBRY UI with new branding to target Japanese English language learners and English language tutors.

    Tutors will be able to publish their own educational material and charge their own rates. Learners will be able to choose their prefered tutor, subject and materials without commiting to a lengthy and expensive corporate curriculum.

    With categories focusing on General Conversation, Exams, Literature, Business and other subject areas, tutors and learners can focus on their chosen area of expertise.

    A free download option will benefit learners by providing free materials and tutors with free advertising while the option to limit downloads with a paywall will ensure tutors an income for their hard work.

    Allow audio/video embeds in article posts and make adding images easier.

    A scheduling page will allow learners to book video meetings and classes through one sleek calendar system while giving tutors the freedom to take charge of their working hours.

    Adding Japanese Yen as a payment option will allow both learners and tutors to save on exchange rates and fees.


  • Stage Two

    Encourage Big Hitters to Sync with Eigo LBRY

    As Eigo LBRY has lower fees and more options and functionality than platforms such as YouTube and Patreon hosts will naturally flock to our platform.

    Just two creators, Atsueigo & Oriental Pearl, have over a million language learners following them. As these and numerous similar creators sync their content to Eigo LBRY a significant percentage of their subscribers will follow.


  • Stage Three

    Invite tutors & learners to sign up to Eigo LBRY

    There are several popular online learning platforms in Japan providing over 1,000,000 English language learners with a choice from over 20,000 tutors. All of these platforms can be significantly improved in various ways. Tutors should be able to create & choose the materials they use and the students they teach. Learners should be able to access materials and tutors which are not heavily restricted by coporate publishers.

    Eigo LBRY solves all of these problems by giving tutors the freedom to create and focus on what they love while choosing the students they work with best. Learners benefit from having the freedom to choose their favourite content and creators on a lesson-by-lesson basis without having to commit to lenghty and expensive contracts.


  • Stage Four

    Hit our first Milestone - Economic Viability

    300 learners taking an average of 15 hours of lessons a month at an average of $10 a lesson would result in a total monthly talk time of 5,000 hours and a turnover of $50,000 a month.

    This would allow an average of 100 tutors working an average of 50 hours a month with approximately 3 regular learners to earn a monthly income of $500.

    A 5% fee for Eigo LBRY and a 5% fee for LBRY developers will cover the projected marketing costs of $2,500/month and the developer costs of a projected $2,500/month.

    After reaching this milestone we can continue to expand to other subjects and other countries until we reach the moon.
